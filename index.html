Elevator Technology

Every Type. Every Building.

We are not limited to one technology. Advanced Point supplies, installs and maintains the full range of modern elevator systems — matching the right solution to every building, budget and requirement.

01
⚙️

Traction Elevators

The gold standard for mid-to-high-rise buildings. Electric motor-driven with steel ropes and a counterweight system — offering smooth, fast and energy-efficient vertical travel for offices, hotels and residential towers.

Commercial & High-Rise
02
💧

Hydraulic Elevators

Powered by a hydraulic piston system, these are ideal for low-to-mid-rise buildings where a machine room is available. Known for their reliability, smooth ride and cost-effective installation in commercial and residential settings.

Low-to-Mid Rise
03
🌀

Vacuum / Pneumatic Elevators

Our signature air-powered technology. Operates on air pressure differentials with no cables, pulleys or pistons — making it perfect for private residences. Functions reliably during power outages, is self-supporting, and requires minimal structural modification.

Residential & Power-Free
04
🪜

Stairlifts

A safe and affordable mobility solution for seniors, children and people with limited mobility. Stairlifts are fitted directly onto existing staircases — straight or curved — enabling independent movement across floors without structural changes to the building.

Accessibility & Mobility
05
🏗️

Machine Room-Less (MRL)

A modern evolution of the traction elevator that eliminates the need for a separate machine room. The drive is housed within the shaft itself — saving space, reducing construction cost and maintaining high performance for mid-rise buildings.

Space-Saving Mid-Rise
06
♿

Platform Lifts & Dumbwaiters

Platform lifts provide wheelchair and disabled access across short vertical distances without a full elevator shaft. Dumbwaiters serve commercial kitchens, hospitals and warehouses for the efficient transport of goods between floors.

Access & Goods Movement
